Welcome to the New Plioverse Wiki!

The Plioverse was an interconnected lore of Nationstates nations, roleplay, and youtube videos. They were the home of The Nelois Confederacy, Pliomopia (It's Namesake), Butthenhagen, Hattenper Dun Gumrebra, New Colian, the infamous Mike Damus, and St. Money Land, among others. They had close ties with the AoR, NROB, and others. This wiki is dedicated to documenting the region's lore from it's 3 year tenure.
